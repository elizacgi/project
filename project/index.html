MISSION STATEMENT

An innovative Network open for corporates and banks 1 which enables financing deeper into global supply chains and offers an easy way to join


The Network aims to address the unmet demand for financing earlier in the supply chain by enabling corporates to easily and securely communicate trade information directly with banks of their choice.

1Banks include supervised financial institutions and could potentially include non-supervised entities active in the financial industry.

POTENTIAL SOLUTION BENEFITS

POTENTIAL BENEFITS FOR SUPPLIERS
  • Improves access to financing
  • Enables additional financing earlier in trade cycle
  • Favorable cost of funding
  • Simplifies IT access (including ERP integration) across multiple banks and buyer programs
POTENTIAL BENEFITS FOR BUYERS
  • Strengthens Supplier relationships
  • Stabilizes supply chain by improving Supplier's financial viability
  • Improves COGS and/or payment terms
  • Simplifies IT access (including ERP integration) across multiple banks
POTENTIAL BENEFITS FOR BANKS
  • Better serves the financing needs of clients across the supply chain
  • Reduces fraud risk
  • Attractive asset class
  • Creates an open industry network, by banks for banks
  • Simple and affordable to join
OVERVIEW (HOW INFORMATION FLOWS)
INFORMATION FLOW

Best-in-class risk mitigation for double financing and fraudulent trade information


Corporates will be able to submit and verify purchase orders and invoices to request trade financing from the banks of their choice. By providing those banks with access to trusted trade information, the Network will help to mitigate the risk of double financing and fraudulent trade information across the industry.


This enables the relevant banks to better assess risks and provide trade financing earlier in the supply chain, including for small and medium-sized enterprises who have traditionally experienced difficulty in accessing trade finance. Banks will provide financing outside of the Network using their existing systems.

ACCESSIBLE

Governance and standardization. Open architecture. Standardized connectivity.


Governance model similar to SWIFT to achieve maximum adoption across the supply chain ecosystem.

Corporates will use a simple one-time registration process which will allow them to connect with all banks on the Network.
